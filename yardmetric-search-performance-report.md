## Summary & What to Focus On

The site is brand new — 7 days of index data is not enough to judge long-term trajectory. The foundations look reasonable: Google is finding and indexing pages, impressions are growing daily, and a handful of pages are approaching competitive positions.

**The three most actionable priorities right now:**

1. **Push /construction/ and /lumber-woodworking/ to page 1.** These pages sit at positions 9 and 20 — they are the closest to earning real clicks. Add more content, internal links, and on-page SEO to these two pages first.

2. **Optimize rubber mulch and paver sand calculators.** The rubber mulch page already hits position 5 for one query and position 42 overall. The paver sand page is at position 22. Both can realistically reach page 1.

3. **Add schema markup.** The Search Appearance file is completely empty — no rich results at all. Implementing FAQ or HowTo schema on calculator pages could improve CTR even at current positions.

The gravel and topsoil calculators are the highest-impression pages but rank around position 70–80. These will take months of authority-building to move. Focus energy on the pages that are already close.
